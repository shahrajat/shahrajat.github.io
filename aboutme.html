I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Netflix, where I build the core Model Inference Platform — the infrastructure that serves ML predictions at scale across recommendations, search, ads, and personalization for 200M+ subscribers worldwide.

Before Netflix, I spent four years at Amazon building distributed systems. During the pandemic, I created Amazon Gather, a virtual events platform, as a side project. It was adopted widely across the company and earned me the Just Do It Award from Jeff Bezos — one of Amazon's most prestigious internal recognitions. Prior to Amazon, I worked on institutional securities technology at Morgan Stanley and interned at Goldman Sachs.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from NC State University, where I published research in educational data mining and computer vision. My B.Tech. is from VNIT, India.

Outside of work, I enjoy skating, board games, reading, and prototyping new ideas. I'm always happy to connect — feel free to reach out.
